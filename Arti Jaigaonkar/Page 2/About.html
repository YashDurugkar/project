About the Homestay

This homestay is situated at a prime location with access to the city centre.

IT is just ten minutes drive from Kune Falls, Lonavala Lake, Narayani Dham Temple and Della Adventure Park

There are also multiple wax museums nearby to visit.

The house is well furnished with all modern amenities like TV, Fridge, Oven, Kitchen etc

Well-equipped air-conditioned rooms with clean washrooms are available for stay. Rooms with balcony provide a beautiful view.
